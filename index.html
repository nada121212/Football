Football

History

Football entered Saudi Arabia in the early 20th century, played initially in schools and local clubs. The Saudi Arabian Football Federation was founded in 1957. The Saudi national team first participated in the World Cup in 1994, reaching the Round of 16. Over the years, Saudi Arabia has become a key football nation in Asia with multiple victories in continental championships.

Major Events

  • Saudi Pro League: The top-tier professional football league in Saudi Arabia, known for its competitive nature.
  • Kings Cup: A prestigious domestic cup competition, featuring the best clubs from across the kingdom.
  • Saudi Super Cup: A domestic competition between the winners of the Saudi Pro League and King Cup.
  • AFC Champions League: The premier continental competition for clubs, where Saudi teams like Al Hilal and Al Nassr have seen success.
